Our Story: Built on New Hampshire Values

Plumbing HVAC Services New Hampshire started in 2009 with a simple premise — New Hampshire homeowners deserved a plumbing and HVAC contractor they could actually trust. No surprise fees. No unnecessary upsells. No excuses.

What began as a two-truck operation in Manchester has grown into a statewide team of licensed technicians serving every corner of the Granite State. We cover everything from Nashua in the south to Laconia in the Lakes Region, from Keene in the Monadnock Region to Portsmouth on the Seacoast.

Every technician on our team is background-checked, licensed by the State of New Hampshire, and trained to the highest standards. We believe in treating your home like our own — because your trust is the only thing that keeps us in business.

Our Mission: Honest Service, Every Time

Our mission is straightforward: deliver expert plumbing and HVAC services across New Hampshire with complete honesty, speed, and craftsmanship. We don't cut corners, and we don't pad invoices.

Before we start any job, we give you a written estimate. When the job is done, there are no surprises on the bill. We explain what we found, what we did, and why — because an informed customer is a happy customer.

That philosophy has earned us a 4.9-star rating across 348 Google reviews and made us the most recommended plumber and HVAC contractor in New Hampshire communities from Manchester to Portsmouth.
