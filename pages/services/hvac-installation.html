Frequently Asked Questions

What HVAC system is best for a New Hampshire home? +
Heat pumps are increasingly the top choice for NH — they efficiently heat AND cool, work well in NH's climate with modern cold-climate models, and qualify for significant NH Saves rebates.
How long does HVAC installation take in NH? +
A standard split system installation typically takes one full day. Complex multi-zone systems or those requiring new ductwork take 2-3 days.
Do you help with NH energy efficiency rebates? +
Yes — we're familiar with NH Saves programs and federal tax credits. We'll help identify which rebates apply to your new HVAC installation.
How often should an HVAC system be replaced in NH? +
Most systems last 15-20 years with proper maintenance. If your system is over 15 years old and requiring frequent repairs, replacement often makes better economic sense.
