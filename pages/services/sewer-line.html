Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my sewer line needs repair in NH? +
Warning signs include multiple slow drains simultaneously, gurgling sounds, sewage odors, and wet patches in your yard over the sewer line path.
What is trenchless sewer repair? +
Trenchless methods repair sewer lines with minimal excavation — either lining the existing pipe from inside, or pulling a new pipe through. This protects landscaping and reduces cost.
How long does sewer line replacement take in NH? +
A full traditional sewer line replacement typically takes 1-3 days depending on length and conditions. Trenchless repair is often completed in one day.
Does homeowners insurance cover sewer line repair in NH? +
Standard homeowners insurance typically does not cover sewer line repair. Some NH insurers offer optional sewer line endorsements. We recommend checking your policy.
