Frequently Asked Questions

How long does water heater installation take in NH? +
Most water heater replacements take 2-4 hours. Our NH plumbers carry common units on their trucks for same-day installation in most cases.
What size water heater do I need for my NH home? +
A family of 4 in NH typically needs a 50-gallon tank heater. We'll recommend the right size based on your household's hot water usage.
Are tankless water heaters worth it in New Hampshire? +
Yes — NH's cold groundwater makes tankless heaters slightly less efficient than in warmer climates, but the energy savings and unlimited hot water are still significant benefits.
Do you offer water heater maintenance in NH? +
Yes. Annual water heater flushing and inspection extends lifespan by years. We offer affordable maintenance plans for NH homeowners.
