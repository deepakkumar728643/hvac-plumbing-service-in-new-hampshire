Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I smell gas in my NH home? +
Leave the building immediately without using any electrical switches. Call 911 from outside, then call us at (603) 555-0100. Do not re-enter until cleared by gas company.
Do gas line installations require permits in NH? +
Yes — all gas line work in New Hampshire requires permits. Our licensed NH plumbers handle all permits and inspections as part of the service.
Can you install a gas line for a new appliance in NH? +
Yes — we commonly install gas lines for new stoves, dryers, fireplaces, generators, and outdoor grills throughout New Hampshire.
How do you detect gas leaks? +
We use electronic gas detection equipment for precise, safe leak location. Never use an open flame to test for gas leaks.
