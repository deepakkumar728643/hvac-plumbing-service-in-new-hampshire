Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if my furnace stops working in NH? +
First, check your thermostat settings and replace the filter if dirty. Check your circuit breaker. If it still won't work, call us at (603) 555-0100 — we respond 24/7.
How often should I service my furnace in New Hampshire? +
Annual furnace tune-ups before heating season (September-October) are strongly recommended for all NH homes. This prevents winter breakdowns and ensures efficiency.
Is it safe to run a cracked heat exchanger? +
No — a cracked heat exchanger can leak carbon monoxide into your home. If we diagnose a cracked heat exchanger, we'll discuss your options immediately.
How long do furnaces last in New Hampshire? +
Most gas furnaces last 15-20 years with regular maintenance. NH's heating demands are high, so proper annual servicing significantly extends furnace life.
