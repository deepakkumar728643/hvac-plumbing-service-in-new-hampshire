Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between snaking and hydro-jetting? +
Snaking breaks up blockages mechanically, while hydro-jetting uses high-pressure water to fully scour the pipe walls clean. Hydro-jetting is more thorough for grease buildup and root intrusion.
How often should drains be cleaned in an NH home? +
Most NH homeowners benefit from annual drain cleaning. Kitchen drains may need more frequent service due to grease buildup.
Can you clear a main sewer line clog in NH? +
Yes — we handle main sewer line blockages throughout New Hampshire using professional hydro-jetting equipment.
Is chemical drain cleaner bad for my pipes? +
Yes. Store-bought chemical cleaners can damage pipes, especially older cast iron and PVC. Professional cleaning is safer and more effective.
