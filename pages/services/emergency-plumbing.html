How fast can you get to my NH home in an emergency? +

Do you charge extra for night and weekend emergency calls? +
Our emergency pricing is transparent and flat-rate. We quote you before beginning work and don't add surprise overtime fees.
What counts as a plumbing emergency? +
Burst pipes, active flooding, sewage backups, no hot water in winter, gas smell with plumbing, and situations causing active water damage are all true emergencies.
What should I do while waiting for the emergency plumber? +
Shut off water at the source or the main. For gas smell, leave the building and call 911 first. Document damage with photos. Keep everyone away from contaminated water.
