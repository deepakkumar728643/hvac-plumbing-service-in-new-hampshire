Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my toilet running constantly? +
The most common causes are a worn flapper, faulty fill valve, or incorrect float setting. Our NH plumbers fix running toilets quickly and affordably.
Can you install a new toilet the same day? +
In most cases, yes. Our NH plumbers carry a selection of quality toilets and can complete installation in 1-2 hours.
How do I know if my toilet has a slow leak? +
Put a few drops of food coloring in the tank. If color appears in the bowl within 15 minutes without flushing, you have a leak.
What toilet brands do you install in NH homes? +
We install all major brands including Kohler, American Standard, TOTO, and more. We'll recommend the best option for your budget and bathroom.
