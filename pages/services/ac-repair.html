Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my AC not cooling my NH home properly? +
Common causes include low refrigerant, dirty coils, a failing compressor, or inadequate system sizing. We diagnose accurately before recommending repairs.
How much does AC repair cost in New Hampshire? +
AC repair costs vary by issue. We provide free estimates before beginning any work — so you know the exact cost upfront.
Should I repair or replace my old AC in NH? +
If your AC is over 12-15 years old and the repair cost exceeds 50% of replacement cost, replacement often makes more sense economically.
Do you service ductless mini-splits in NH? +
Yes — we service all major mini-split brands including Mitsubishi, Daikin, LG, Fujitsu, and more throughout New Hampshire.
