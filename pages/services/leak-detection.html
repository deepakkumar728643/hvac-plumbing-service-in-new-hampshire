Leak Detection Services in New Hampshire

Advanced non-invasive leak detection technology locates hidden leaks precisely — saving your NH home from water damage without unnecessary demolition.

📞 Call (603) 555-0100 — FREE Estimate

Hidden water leaks cause enormous damage to New Hampshire homes before they're ever discovered. A slow leak behind a wall or under a slab can cause structural damage, mold growth, and spike your water bill significantly. Plumbing HVAC Services New Hampshire uses advanced non-invasive leak detection technology to find leaks precisely — without tearing apart your home.

Our NH leak detection specialists use acoustic listening devices, thermal imaging, and moisture meters to pinpoint the exact location of leaks in walls, under floors, and underground — so repair is minimally invasive and maximally effective.

Our Leak Detection Services in NH Include:

  • ✅ Acoustic leak detection
  • ✅ Thermal imaging / infrared leak detection
  • ✅ Slab leak detection
  • ✅ Underground water line leak detection
  • ✅ Pool and irrigation leak detection
  • ✅ Water meter leak check
  • ✅ Hidden pipe leak location
  • ✅ Water damage assessment
  • ✅ Leak repair after detection
  • ✅ Pressure testing for leaks

Why Choose Us for 💧 Leak Detection Services?

NH's freeze-thaw cycles are a major cause of hidden pipe leaks. Water freezes in pipe joints, creating micro-fractures that slowly worsen. Many NH homeowners don't discover these leaks until significant damage has occurred. Early detection saves thousands in repairs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I have a hidden water leak in my NH home? +
Signs include unexplained high water bills, damp spots on walls or floors, musty odors, and the sound of running water when nothing is on.
Is leak detection destructive to my home? +
No — our non-invasive methods use acoustic and thermal technology to find leaks without cutting walls or floors first.
Can you detect leaks under concrete slabs in NH? +
Yes. We specialize in slab leak detection using acoustic amplification to pinpoint leaks under concrete foundations.
How much does leak detection cost in New Hampshire? +
We provide free estimates. Leak detection pricing depends on the type and complexity of the suspected leak.
